Spider man

A real Hero.

Spider-Man is one of the most popular and beloved superheroes in the world. Created by "Writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko", he first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. The story follows "Peter Parker", an ordinary teenager who gains extraordinary powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. With his new abilities — wall-crawling, super strength, and spider-sense — Peter decides to fight crime and protect the innocent. Guided by his uncle’s famous words, “With great power comes great responsibility,” Spider-Man represents courage, kindness, and the importance of doing what’s right, even when it’s hard.
